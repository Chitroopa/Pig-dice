Pig Dice

How to play

- The game of Pig is pretty simple. Roll as many times as you want in a turn...but if you get a "1" (a Pig) you lose your turn (and any points earned in that turn).
- Your goal is to get to 100 points before our supercomputer Mr. Watson does.
- So roll away...then click on Hold when you are ready to let Mr. Watson play.
